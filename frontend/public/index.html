About Africa Resource Center

Africa Resource Center was born from our team's firsthand experience with the challenges of accessing quality education in rural African communities. We witnessed the potential of countless bright minds hindered by the lack of resources. This inspired us to create a platform that bridges the education gap, bringing world-class learning materials to every corner of Africa.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, developed over an intensive 4-week period. It represents our commitment to leveraging technology for social good.
